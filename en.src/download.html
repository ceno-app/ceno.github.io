Alternatively, you can install Ceno via F-Droid, the Paskoocheh Marketplace, Github, dComms, or download the APK directly via GitLab:

Important:

Ceno has two modes of operation - Public and Personal. You can easily toggle between them. Public mode offers the best connectivity but the least privacy - websites that you visit or share are recorded in a publicly-accessible registry (BitTorrent). Personal mode eliminates this record but may be slower and less efficient at retrieving content. See the FAQ or User Manual for more details on Ceno usage.
