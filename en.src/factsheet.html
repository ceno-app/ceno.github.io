Colors

UI Colors

For accessibility and readability reasons, here’s the extended Color Palette of 10 different shades of both brand colors. When colors are used in text, according to Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2, Level AA requires the contrast ratio to be at least 4.5:1. Any color of the palette can be used if this requirement is met.

Typography

Metropolis is used as primary font of the visual identity. It can be used in lower and upper cases. It comes with a family of styles. This font is used in texts such as titles and headers.

Inter is used as secondary font of the visual identity. It can be used in lower and upper cases. It comes with a family of styles. This font is used in texts such as body texts.
